    Satchel is a mod-manager with the goal of making the handling of mods as easy as possible. Satchel can auto-update, install, and configurate all of your mods for all of your games in one place. Satchel is sadly not currently out yet, but is being worked on. Satchel will eventually have its own page on this site.
