    Satchel

    Satchel is a mod-manager with the goal of making the handling of mods as easy as possible. Satchel can auto-update, install, and configurate all of your mods for all of your games in one place.

    Features

    • Automatic mod updating
    • Support for a wide range of games
    • User-friendly GUI
    • Config editing
    • Makes installing mods easy
